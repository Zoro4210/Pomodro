Our Philosophy

Focus isn't forced.
It's cultivated.

We live in a world of constant pings, notifications, and context-switching. Pomodro was born from a need for sanctuary — a digital space where the only thing that matters is the task at hand.

By leveraging tactile feedback, fluid animations, and a strict orientation-based UX paradigm, Pomodro physically requires you to shift your device to shift your mindset.
